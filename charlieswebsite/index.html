  • Charlie's Cafe, one of Sun Devil Dining's local restaurant partners, brings the taste of Europe to ASU! With three locations on campus, there is always a Charlie's Cafe near! Charlie's Cafe is located at the Hayden Library, Bio Design B Building and the College of Design.
